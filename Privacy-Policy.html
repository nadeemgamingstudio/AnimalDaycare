🐣 Privacy overview

Animal Daycare is a heartwarming simulation where players care for adorable pets — feed them, play with toys, groom and make them happy. We value your privacy as much as we love animals. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how you stay in control.

Our commitment: Zero data selling, full child safety compliance (COPPA / GDPR-K), and transparent practices. Your trust is our top priority.

🐾 1. What data we collect

We only gather essential information to keep the daycare running smoothly and improve your experience:

  • 📱 Device & technical info — model, OS version, language, country, advertising identifier (AAID / IDFA), IP address. Used for analytics and ad functionality.
  • 🎮 Game progress & preferences — pets adopted, items collected, level progression, sound/vibration settings. Stored locally on your device (no mandatory login).
  • 📢 Ad interaction data — via Google AdMob. AdMob may use identifiers to serve contextual or personalized ads (with respect to child settings).
  • 🍼 In-app purchases — if you buy special toys or remove ads, Google Play Billing processes the payment. We never see your card details.

We never request real names, contacts, location (beyond general region), or media files.

🐾 2. How we use your data

Your information helps us provide a safe and fun daycare environment:

  • Save game progress: remember your pets, unlocked items, and daily rewards.
  • Analyze & improve performance — using Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics to fix bugs and optimize gameplay.
  • Show advertisements (optional rewarded videos for extra coins) via Google AdMob.
  • Verify in-app purchases and grant virtual goods.
We do not share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

🐾 3. Third‑party services (SDKs)

To deliver core features, we rely on trusted partners:

  • Google AdMob – advertising network. May collect device data and IP for relevant ads. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics – aggregated usage data and crash reports. Helps us keep Animal Daycare stable and fun.
  • Google Play Billing – secure handling of any in-app purchases.

All partners comply with GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, and other applicable regulations.

🐾 4. Data retention & deletion

Your pet daycare progress stays on your device. Uninstalling the app will remove local progress. No account is required. To request deletion of any data linked to your advertising ID or analytics, email us at nadeemgamer180@gmail.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

You may reset your advertising ID via device settings (Android: Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID).

🐾 5. Your ad choices & opt‑out

We give you control over personalized ads:

  • On Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • For users under the age of consent (region-specific), only non-personalized contextual ads are served (COPPA mode).
  • Rewarded video ads are always voluntary — watch them for in-game bonuses only if you choose.
Child-safe design enabled & COPPA compliant

🐾 6. Children's privacy (COPPA / GDPR‑K)

Animal Daycare is designed for families and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA).

  • No personal data (name, email) is required to enjoy the game.
  • If the Play Store identifies a child account, personalized ads and certain analytics are automatically disabled.
  • Parents or guardians: if you believe your child has provided any personal data (e.g., by contacting support), please email us and we will delete it immediately.

🐾 7. Data security

We implement industry best practices: encrypted connections (TLS), limited data retention, and strict access controls. Although no system is 100% immune, we continuously update our security measures to protect your privacy.

🐾 8. Your privacy rights

Depending on your location (EEA, UK, California, Brazil, India), you may have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold (via advertising ID correlation).
  • Request correction or deletion of data.
  • Opt-out of the "sale" of personal data (we never sell).
  • Withdraw consent for analytics by resetting your advertising ID.

To exercise any right, email nadeemgamer180@gmail.com with subject “Privacy Request – Animal Daycare”. We will reply within a reasonable timeframe.

🐾 9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. If significant changes occur, we will notify you via an in-game notice or update the effective date on this page. Continued use of Animal Daycare means you accept the revised policy.
